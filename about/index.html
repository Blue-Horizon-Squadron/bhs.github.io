Our History

Blue Horizon Squadron was founded in {{ site.squadron.founded }} by a group of DCS enthusiasts who wanted more than casual online flying. We wanted structured operations, realistic tactics, and a community that cared about the craft of virtual aviation.

How Operations Work

Operations are planned and published on the Operations page 1–2 weeks in advance. Pilots register through the website, selecting their preferred role and aircraft. Once registered, you'll receive a full briefing package via Discord — including mission files, frequencies, waypoints, and rules of engagement.

On the night, we brief in Discord voice 30 minutes before engine start. Operations typically last 2–4 hours and are followed by a debrief where we review the mission and improve for next time.
