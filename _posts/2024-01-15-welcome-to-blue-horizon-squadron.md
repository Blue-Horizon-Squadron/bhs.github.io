Attention all pilots — welcome to the official Blue Horizon Squadron website.

After months of planning, we're proud to launch our new home on the web. This site will serve as the central hub for everything BHS:

- **Upcoming Operations** — register for missions, pick your role, and receive your briefing package
- **Member Roster** — see who's flying with us
- **News & Updates** — mission reports, announcements, and community highlights
- **Discord Integration** — all registrations feed directly into our Discord server

## What's Next

Our first major operation is scheduled for **5 April 2026** — *Operation Blue Dawn* over the Caucasus map. Slots are filling up fast, so head to the [Operations page](/operations/) and register now.

## How Registration Works

1. Go to the [Operations page](/operations/)
2. Find the operation you want to join
3. Click **Register for this Operation**
4. Fill in your Discord username, callsign, and preferred role
5. Submit — you'll receive the full briefing package via Discord DM

It's that simple. No spreadsheets, no DMs to leadership, just click and fly.

## Discord

Everything else happens on Discord — voice comms during ops, debriefs, training sessions, and general squadron banter. If you haven't joined yet, click the Discord button in the nav bar.

*Blue Horizon Squadron — Fly High. Fight Hard. Fly Together.*
